Our Process

Hiring Process

What to expect when you apply for a position at NMSWorks

Application Review

Our recruitment team carefully reviews your application, resume, and cover letter to assess your qualifications and fit for the role.

Tips:
  • Tailor your resume to highlight relevant skills and experience
  • Include specific achievements and metrics where possible
  • Ensure your application is complete and error-free

Initial Screening

A brief phone or video call with our recruiter to discuss your background, experience, and expectations for the role.

Tips:
  • Research NMSWorks and our products before the call
  • Prepare concise answers about your experience and skills
  • Have questions ready about the role and company culture

Technical Assessment

Depending on the role, you may be asked to complete a technical assessment, coding challenge, or case study to demonstrate your skills.

Tips:
  • Read instructions carefully and ask for clarification if needed
  • Focus on clean, well-documented code or solutions
  • Explain your thought process and approach

Team Interviews

A series of interviews with potential team members, managers, and other stakeholders to assess technical skills, cultural fit, and alignment with our values.

Tips:
  • Prepare examples that demonstrate your technical and soft skills
  • Be ready to discuss how you handle challenges and collaborate with others
  • Ask thoughtful questions about the team, projects, and work environment

Offer & Onboarding

If selected, you'll receive a job offer with details about compensation, benefits, and start date. Upon acceptance, our HR team will guide you through the onboarding process.

Tips:
  • Review the offer carefully and ask any questions you may have
  • Complete onboarding paperwork promptly
  • Prepare for your first day by reviewing any materials provided

Helpful Advice

Application Tips

Maximize your chances of success with these application tips

Tailor Your Resume

Customize your resume for each position you apply for, highlighting relevant skills and experience. Use specific examples and metrics to demonstrate your achievements.

  • Match keywords from the job description
  • Quantify achievements with numbers when possible
  • Keep it concise and well-organized
  • Proofread carefully for errors

Research NMSWorks

Take time to understand our company, products, and culture before applying. This knowledge will help you tailor your application and prepare for interviews.

  • Explore our website and social media
  • Read about our products and solutions
  • Understand our company values
  • Research recent news and developments

Prepare for Interviews

Practice common interview questions and prepare specific examples that demonstrate your skills and experience relevant to the position.

  • Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
  • Prepare questions to ask the interviewer
  • Practice technical questions if applicable
  • Test your video conferencing setup for remote interviews
