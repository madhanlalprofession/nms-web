Executive Leadership

Meet Our Executive Team

The visionaries guiding NMSWorks to new heights

Dr. Rajesh Kumar

Chief Executive Officer

Dr. Kumar brings over 25 years of experience in the telecom industry, having previously led major digital transformation initiatives at global telecom operators. He holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from MIT and has been recognized with numerous industry awards for his visionary leadership.

"Our mission is to transform the telecom landscape through innovative solutions that empower operators to deliver exceptional service to their customers."

Dr. Priya Sharma

Chief Technology Officer

Dr. Sharma leads our technology strategy and innovation initiatives. With a background in network architecture and AI, she has pioneered several breakthrough technologies in telecom OSS/BSS systems. She holds a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Stanford University.

"Technology should solve real problems. At NMSWorks, we focus on developing solutions that address the complex challenges facing modern telecom operators."

Michael Chen

Chief Operating Officer

Michael oversees our global operations, ensuring efficient delivery of our solutions to customers worldwide. With 20 years of experience in operational excellence and business transformation, he has a proven track record of scaling technology companies.

"Operational excellence is the foundation of customer satisfaction. We strive to deliver flawless execution in everything we do."
